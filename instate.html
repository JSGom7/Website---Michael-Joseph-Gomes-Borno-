Borno's In-State Outings!

Indianapolis Zoo and Children's Museum

Borno visited Indianapolis Zoo twice. First visit was during summer 2017 with his grandma when he was only three months old. He also visited Children's Museum of Indianapolis on that day. He visited the zoo again in summer 2018.

Happy Hollow Park

Borno went to Happy Hollow Park in West Lafayette to enjoy the summer picnic arranged by Purdue Bangladesh Student's Association in July 2017.

Indiana Beach Amusement and Water Park

Borno attended his dad's office picnic at Indiana Beach Amusement and Water Park in Monticello during summer 2017. He was only four months old then.

Ross Hills Park

Borno enjoyed a Fall BBQ at Ross Hill Park in West Lafayette arranged by Purdue Bangladesh Student's Association in Fall 2017. He was too young to enjoy the BBQ though.
