Borno's Out-of-State Tours!

Chicago, Illinois

Borno visited Chicago three times. First visit was to see off his grandparents at O'hare International Airport. The rest two visits were to attend family wedding.

Louisville, Kentucky

Borno went to Louisville to meet his Aunt Ruponti and Uncle Subrata twice. First visit was in November 2017 and the second one was in September 2018.

Arizona

In December 2017 during Christmas break Borno went to visit The Grand Canyon National Park in Arizona. He was nine months old then.

Las Vegas, Navada

Borno visited famous Hoover Dam and Las Vegas strip during his Christmas tour in 2017. He was too young to enjoy the vegas life though.
