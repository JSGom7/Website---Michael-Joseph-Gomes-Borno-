Borno's Out-of-State Tours!

Maryland

In Summer 2018 Borno paid a visit to Maryland and spent good time with his maternal relatives. He really enjoyed his time there.

Virginia

Borno went to see The Great Falls Park in Virginia along with his family and relatives in May 2018. After the sight-seeing he had a picnic lunch there with all others.

Tennesse

Borno visited The Great Smoky Mountain National Park during Labor Day Weekend in 2018. He also visited the Titanic Museum in Pigeon Forge, TN during that visit.
