Borno's In-State Outings!

Indiana Dunes Beach

Borno went all the way towards Northern Indiana to visit Indiana Dunes Beach twice. His first visit was in September 2017 and the second one in July 2018.

Turkey Run State Park

Borno enjoyed a picnic in Turkey Run State Park in Marshall arranged and attended by fellow Bangladeshis residing in Indiana.

Mounds Campground, Brookville Lake

Borno went for his first camping when he was fifteen months old. It was during summer 2018 and the location was Mounds Campground, Brookville Lake in Franklin.

Brown County State Park

In November 2018 Borno visited Brown County State Park in Southern Indiana to enjoy the beautiful fall colour of nature.
